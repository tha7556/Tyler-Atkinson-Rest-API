About

I began my programming journey in my high school robotics class, where we programmed robots to drive around and pick up objects using a Java-esque language. Based on a mentor's recommendation, I continued to pursue a new-found coding passion in AP Computer Science, where the basics of Object-Oriented Design and Java were introduced. I continued on to study Computer Science at the University of North Carolina Wilmington where I worked with Java, Python, C/C++, C#, SQL, and many other languages and technologies. After Graduating I was hired by Qualitest as a Quality Assurance Engineer where I used Java, Selinium, and other testing frameworks to test websites and web APIs.
